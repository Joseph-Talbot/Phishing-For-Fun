Introduction

Welcome to this test of most of your phishing prevention skills.


    First of all I would like to thank you for taking this test of your own free will *cough* *cough*.
Anyways you as the test subject will be trying to determine if an email is a phishing email or a real email.
Here are a few things to look for as you answer the prompts:

 1. Look for the second half of the From email, they normally dont match who is actually supposed to be sending the email.

 2. If there are a lot of typos or interesting slips in grammer.

 3. Asking for banking/credit card information.

 4. Asking for money.

 5. If there are links that look in any way, renamed or tampered with.

 6. If it wasnt already obvious if the first two of the previous points are true never open a link.

 7. If the third and fourth are coupled with either one or two, the same as five applies.

 8. If they ask you to forward the email to some you know.

 9. If they address you BY YOUR FULL NAME and they shouldn't know that information.
